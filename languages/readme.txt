LANGUAGE TRANSLATION FILES FOR IMCLARITY
---------------------------------------

If you are interested in creating a language translation for Imclarity then
you're in the right place!  We would love your help, and you
can get started translating Imclarity at https://translate.wordpress.org/projects/wp-plugins/imclarity

Anything you can do to help is greatly appreciated and allows
Imclarity to be more easily used by people all over the world.

Thank you for supporting Imclarity and all free software!
